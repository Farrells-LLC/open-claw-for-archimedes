# Marketing Review

Tags: #directive #marketing #archimedes

Use when marketing files, reports, screenshots, trackers, outreach drafts, campaign assets, or positioning notes are posted.

## Steps

1. Identify what artifact was posted and where it fits in [[second-brain/projects/archimedes-marketing]].
2. Review for audience clarity, business value, factual accuracy, and next-action usefulness.
3. Note concrete decisions or follow-ups in the project hub or daily memory when they are worth keeping.
4. Keep channel replies concise: findings first, then next steps.

## Watch For

- Claims that need proof
- Confusing positioning
- Missing call to action
- Weak customer language
- Tracker fields that do not support follow-up
- Assets that are polished but not useful for sales
