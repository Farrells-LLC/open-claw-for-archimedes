# Archimedes Customer One-Pager - 2026-07-11

## Turn Messy Business Exports Into Action Reports

Archimedes helps ecommerce and SaaS teams turn raw operational data into focused reports that explain what happened, where risk is concentrated, and what to review first.

## The Problem

Teams often have the data, but it is trapped in messy exports:

- Shopify and merchant reports.
- SKU and inventory planning sheets.
- Customer health and churn files.
- Support and onboarding exports.
- Demo datasets and reporting prep files.

Dashboards help once the metrics are clean. Archimedes is useful earlier, when the team still needs to understand what the messy file is saying.

## What Archimedes Produces

- Clear executive summary.
- Priority segments.
- Risk drivers.
- Action queues.
- Operating rules.
- Next-step recommendations.

## Example: Ecommerce Stockout And Margin

Archimedes analyzed a 7,200-row synthetic SKU-week dataset and produced a focused report around stockouts caused by discount-led demand.

Key outputs:

- 37.2% stockout rate.
- $3.0M estimated lost revenue.
- Promo readiness rule: do not launch 20%+ discounts unless inventory covers at least 1.25x forecast demand.

## Example: SaaS Churn Recovery

Archimedes analyzed a 6,500-row synthetic account dataset and found support pressure acting as a churn signal.

Key outputs:

- 12.1% baseline 90-day churn risk.
- 29.7% churn for accounts with 8+ support tickets.
- Rescue queue based on ticket volume, unresolved issues, onboarding score, time-to-value, and revenue impact.

## Best Fit

Archimedes is a good fit when:

- Your team has recurring messy exports.
- Reporting prep takes too much manual cleanup.
- You need a fast first analyst pass.
- You want an operating recommendation, not just a chart.

## Not A Fit Yet

Archimedes is not the right first tool when:

- You need a governed enterprise BI replacement.
- You need fully automated decisions with no human review.
- You are working with regulated medical data in an outbound context.
