Specific Issue

Support burden is acting like an early churn alarm, not a healthy engagement signal. Accounts with 8 or more tickets in 90 days churn at 29.7%, compared with 8.9% for lower-ticket accounts. The strongest risk pocket is Growth-plan ecommerce accounts that combine low onboarding, long time-to-value, and unresolved support volume.

What To Fix First

Do not start with a broad retention campaign. Start with a rescue queue: Growth and Scale accounts with 8+ support tickets, onboarding below 55, and more than 21 days to first value. These accounts are close enough to revenue to matter and specific enough for customer success to act on.
