Specific Issue

Promotions are creating demand that inventory cannot satisfy. The dataset shows 2,676 stockout SKU-weeks and $3.0M in estimated lost revenue. This is not a broad sales problem; it is a planning problem around high-velocity discounted SKUs.

What To Fix First

Pause broad discounting on categories where stockout risk is already above 45%. Move those SKUs into a replenishment watchlist before the next promotion cycle, especially Shopify and Amazon rows with 20%+ discounts.
