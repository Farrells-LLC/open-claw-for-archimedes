# Archimedes Growth Experiment Backlog - 2026-07-11

## Experiment 1 - Ecommerce Proof Point

Hypothesis:

Ecommerce teams will respond better to a concrete stockout/margin example than to generic messy-data language.

Asset:

Ecommerce stockout screenshot and post.

Success:

At least one reply, comment, DM, or example request from the first proof-point post or outreach batch.

## Experiment 2 - SaaS Churn Rescue Queue

Hypothesis:

SaaS teams will understand the value faster when Archimedes is framed as a way to create a customer success rescue queue.

Asset:

SaaS churn screenshot and post.

Success:

At least one founder/operator repeats the phrase "support pressure," "churn queue," or similar pain language back.

## Experiment 3 - Manual Outreach With Example Offer

Hypothesis:

Manual, respectful outreach asking whether messy exports are a real pain will outperform a direct product pitch.

Asset:

10-lead first batch outreach CSV.

Success:

2 meaningful replies or example requests from 10 careful messages.

## Experiment 4 - Demo Page CTA

Hypothesis:

"See an example report" will feel safer than "Send a messy export" for first-time visitors.

Asset:

Static demo share page.

Success:

Prospects ask for the example report or engage without needing a long explanation.

## Experiment 5 - Segment Split

Hypothesis:

Ecommerce analytics and B2B ecommerce SaaS will respond better than generic Shopify app teams.

Asset:

Lead list categories and weekly scorecard.

Success:

One segment has a noticeably higher positive reply rate after 20-30 touches.

## Kill Criteria

- No meaningful replies after 30 careful manual touches across multiple segments.
- Prospects consistently say the problem is already solved by existing workflow.
- Prospects only want dashboards and do not care about action reports.

## Pivot Options

- Move from ecommerce/SaaS vendors to internal operators.
- Offer one-off report preparation as a service wrapper.
- Focus on demo dataset/report generation for SaaS vendors.
- Build deeper product support for a specific workflow like stockout/margin or churn rescue.
