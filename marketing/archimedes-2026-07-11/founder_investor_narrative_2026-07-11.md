# Archimedes Founder / Investor Narrative - 2026-07-11

## One-Sentence Company Story

Archimedes turns messy operational data into action reports for teams that need useful decisions before they have clean dashboards.

## Why Now

Every company has more exports, SaaS tools, merchant platforms, support systems, and customer data than it can cleanly interpret. AI makes the first analyst pass cheaper, but generic chat interfaces still leave teams with vague answers, messy workflows, and weak follow-through.

The opportunity is not "ask questions about a CSV." The opportunity is to productize the messy middle between raw data and a decision.

## The Wedge

Start with ecommerce and SaaS because:

- The pain is common and concrete.
- The data is usually tabular.
- Existing Archimedes examples already match the use case.
- The current lead list is aligned with ecommerce/SaaS vendors and operators.
- Non-medical examples reduce compliance and messaging risk.

## The Product Insight

Users do not just need charts. They need:

- What happened.
- Which segment matters.
- What rule should change.
- Who should be reviewed first.
- Whether the data is clean enough to trust.

Archimedes should become the practical first-pass analyst for this workflow.

## Early Proof Assets

- Ecommerce stockout/margin demo: 7,200 SKU-week rows, $3.0M estimated lost revenue, promo readiness rule.
- SaaS churn demo: 6,500 account rows, 29.7% churn in high-ticket accounts, rescue queue recommendation.
- QA evidence: 5/5 structured dataset workflows completed end-to-end.
- Lead list: 50 ecommerce/SaaS prospects, including 36 Priority A leads.

## Business Model Hypotheses

1. One-off action report for a messy export.
2. Monthly reporting prep subscription.
3. SaaS/vendor demo dataset and report prep service.
4. Productized internal analyst workflow for recurring data operations.

## Strategic Risk

The company will lose if it sounds like generic AI analytics. It wins if prospects can immediately picture the painful workflow: "I have a messy export and need a useful report by tomorrow."

## Near-Term Milestone

Get 10 careful manual touches into the market and learn whether ecommerce/SaaS teams repeat the pain back in their own words.
