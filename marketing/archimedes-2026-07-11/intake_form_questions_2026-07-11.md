# Messy Export Intake Form Questions - 2026-07-11

Use these before asking anyone to share data. Keep the first version short.

## Required Questions

1. What kind of data is this?
   - Ecommerce orders/SKUs/inventory.
   - SaaS accounts/customers.
   - Support/onboarding.
   - Reporting or demo prep.
   - Other.

2. What decision are you trying to make from the file?

3. What is currently painful about using this export?

4. Which column or outcome matters most, if any?

5. Are there columns that should not be used because they are sensitive, misleading, or not available in real life?

6. Is the data synthetic, anonymized, or real business data?

7. Are there any regulated, medical, financial, or personal-data concerns?

8. What would make the output useful?
   - Executive summary.
   - Risk segments.
   - Priority queue.
   - Operating rule.
   - Cleanup notes.
   - Charts.
   - Other.

## Safety Notes

- Do not ask for regulated medical data in this campaign.
- Encourage synthetic or anonymized samples first.
- Do not promise automated decisions.
- Treat the report as a review aid, not a final authority.

## Internal Triage

Accept first:

- Ecommerce stockout/margin/inventory files.
- SaaS churn/support/onboarding files.
- Demo/reporting prep files.

Defer:

- Regulated healthcare.
- High-risk credit decisions.
- Files with obvious sensitive personal data.
- Requests requiring production integrations before a manual report proves value.
