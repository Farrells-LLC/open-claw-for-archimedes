## Tracking Checklist

Before first post/outreach:

- Confirm the preferred public URL for example reports.
- Decide whether replies should go to email, form, or DM.
- Add internal testing devices/IPs to the internal traffic exclusions tab.
- Create one campaign row for "July ecommerce/SaaS manual test."
- Create one content row for each LinkedIn post.
- Use simple UTM names:
  - campaign: `july-ecommerce-saas-manual-test`
  - source: `linkedin`, `email`, or `manual-form`
  - medium: `organic-social`, `manual-outreach`, or `direct`

Daily review:

- Posts published.
- Manual messages sent.
- Replies received.
- Example report requests.
- Qualified inquiries.
- Notes on objections or unclear wording.

Do not optimize yet:

- Do not change the whole pitch after one non-response.
- Do not buy paid tools before manual signal.
- Do not count internal testing as traction.
- Do not send bulk automated email.

When to revise:

- After 5-10 manual sends with no replies, test a sharper subject line.
- After 2+ confused replies, simplify the one-liner.
- After any positive reply, capture the exact phrase they used for the pain point.
