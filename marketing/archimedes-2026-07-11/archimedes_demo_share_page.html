Built For The First Useful Analyst Pass

Archimedes is not trying to replace every BI workflow. It is built for the earlier, messier moment when the export exists but the decision is still unclear.

  1. 1 Upload messy operating data.Use CSVs, exports, merchant reports, support data, customer-health files, or SKU-level planning sheets.
  2. 2 Choose the business question.Churn risk, margin leakage, stockout risk, reporting prep, demo datasets, and similar structured workflows.
  3. 3 Review the action report.Get concentrated risks, priority segments, operating rules, and the next queue your team should inspect.
