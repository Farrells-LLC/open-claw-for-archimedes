The Process

Five steps.
Permanent intelligence.

Organize your data first. Then understand it. Then train a model that works forever.

01

✦ Organize Your Data

Have messy files from multiple sources? Upload them all. Archimedes MD merges, cleans, and structures them into one coherent dataset automatically. Skip this if your data is already clean.

02

Upload Your Data

Drop any structured dataset — CSV, JSON, Excel, ZIP, or IPYNB. Archimedes MD reads your columns, detects the task type, and identifies patterns automatically.

03

✦ Get Your Data Intelligence Report

Before training anything, receive a full professional analysis — executive summary, statistical deep dive, correlations, segment breakdowns, outlier profiling, effect sizes, and 8+ prioritized business recommendations. In just a few minutes.

04

AI Selects & Trains the Model

The system tests multiple algorithms — Random Forest, Gradient Boosting, Logistic Regression — picks the best one for your data, and trains it with real accuracy metrics you can trust.

05

Call It Forever

Save the trained model to your library. Whenever a similar problem arises — a new record, transaction, or measurement — the model evaluates it instantly and explains what drove the result.

Capabilities

Built for every domain.
Mastered for yours.

✦ Data Organization

Have data scattered across multiple files, spreadsheets, or systems? Upload them all at once. Archimedes MD analyzes every file, proposes a merge plan for your review, then builds one clean coherent dataset — ready to train a model or generate a report instantly. Free users get 5 sessions total; paid plans get significantly more.

✦ Data Intelligence Report

Upload a dataset and get a full professional analysis in a few minutes — executive summary, statistical deep dive, correlation analysis, segment breakdowns, effect sizes, and 8+ prioritized business recommendations with specific first steps. Valuable on its own, even before training a model.

Auto Strategy Selection

Archimedes MD tests multiple algorithms on your data and automatically picks the one with the highest real accuracy. No guesswork, no data science degree required.

Persistent Model Library

Every trained model is saved permanently to your account. Return months later and run new data through a model trained on thousands of past cases.

Production Python Output

Every model ships as a clean, runnable Python file. Install the dependencies, point it at your data, and run it locally. No cloud lock-in, ever.

Feature Importance & Explanations

Every prediction comes with a ranked feature importance chart showing which columns drove the result, plus a plain English summary you can actually act on.
