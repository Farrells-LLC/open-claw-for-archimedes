Messy ecommerce organizer test pack

Known messiness:
- customer_id appears as customer, Customer ID, and lowercase IDs
- sku appears as sku and SKU with case differences
- dates use multiple formats
- unit_price and refund_amount mix numbers and currency strings
- channel/category/region values have aliases and blanks
- orders contain intentional duplicates
- refunds are JSON, inventory is TSV, most others are CSV

Suggested organizer goal:
Merge into one clean ecommerce dataset suitable for stockout/lost revenue analysis. Preserve leading zeros in customer IDs.
